About Us

Shagunam Micro Association (SMA) is a social development organization focused on uplifting underserved communities across India. With a mission to empower individuals through education and promote fields like commerce, arts, science, sports, social welfare, and environmental protection, SMA aims to create opportunities for growth and positive change.

Shagunam actively engages in forums, seminars, and camps to spread awareness on healthy habits, moral values, and self-confidence. The organization is committed to sustainable development, building local capacity, and fostering community-based leadership to drive meaningful social impact across diverse communities.
